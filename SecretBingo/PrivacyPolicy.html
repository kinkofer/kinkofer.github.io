Privacy Policy

App: Secret Bingo
Last Updated: December 28, 2025

In short: Secret Bingo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data whatsoever. Your privacy is completely protected.

Overview

Prismatic Games ("we" or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices for the Secret Bingo mobile application.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Secret Bingo does not collect any information from you, including but not limited to:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers, etc.)
  • Device information (device IDs, IP addresses, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Game statistics or preferences
  • Photos, contacts, or other device data
  • Any other form of personal or non-personal information

How the App Works

Secret Bingo operates entirely locally on your device. All game data, including your game progress, settings, and preferences, are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Secret Bingo does not use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services
  • Crash reporting tools

The app functions completely independently without connecting to the internet or any external services.

Data Security

Since Secret Bingo does not collect or transmit any data, there is no data that could be intercepted, accessed, or compromised. Your gaming experience is private and secure.

Children's Privacy

Secret Bingo does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union). The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make any changes that affect how we handle data (such as introducing data collection), we will notify users by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, if significant changes are made, through an in-app notification.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data from you, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over all aspects of your use of the app.
